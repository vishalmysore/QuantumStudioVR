🌌 Welcome to Quantum VR

How to use:

  1. Tap "Enter AR" and grant camera permissions
  2. Point your phone at a flat surface (floor, table)
  3. Tap the screen or "Place" button to place the Bloch sphere
  4. Walk around the sphere to see it from different angles
  5. Use quantum gate buttons to manipulate the qubit state
  6. Watch the purple dot move as the quantum state evolves!

💡 The Bloch sphere visualizes a single qubit. The vertical axis represents |0⟩ (top) and |1⟩ (bottom).
